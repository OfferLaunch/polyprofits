Our Story

From watching prediction markets by hand to building a bot that runs 24/7 on Polymarket. This is why we built PolyProfits—and why we're making automated trading accessible to everyone.

What We Do

PolyProfits is a fully automated Polymarket bot that trades on your behalf. You connect your account, set your preferences once—risk level, markets, limits—and the system runs 24/7. No screens to watch, no daily decisions to make.

We handle execution, position management, and monitoring. You get exposure to real-world outcome markets—elections, events, and more—without the manual work. Set it and forget it.
